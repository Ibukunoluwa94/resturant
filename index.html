    Our History

    Ademi Cafe was first organized as a buying club in November of 1972. Members met in each others’ homes to prepare orders and to divide up bulk shipments. Foods included organic and other unadulterated, minimally processed whole foods.

    Within a few months, the Co-op had outgrown people’s living rooms, and in 1973, it moved downtown to the third floor of the YWCA on North Mill Street.

    This was the first of six moves for our Co-op which included our next move to a service garage on Ashland Avenue.
